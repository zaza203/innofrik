About Innofrik

Connecting businesses with global talent since 2024

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Innofrik began as a small team of IT professionals with a vision to bridge the gap between global talent and businesses seeking quality outsourcing solutions.

What started as a modest operation has grown into a leading global outsourcing provider with teams of talented and experience engineers.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, an unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep understanding of our clients' evolving needs.
